Our Multi-Model Approach to Predictive Maintenance

Bearing Fault Detection Model

This specialized model analyzes vibration patterns to detect early signs of bearing degradation and failure. Using advanced signal processing and machine learning algorithms, it identifies characteristic frequency patterns associated with bearing faults.

Misalignment Detection Model

Our misalignment detection system monitors current signatures and temperature variations to identify shaft and coupling misalignments. This model helps prevent secondary damage and energy inefficiency caused by improper alignment.

General Fault Classification Model

This comprehensive classifier identifies other potential faults beyond bearing and misalignment issues. It analyzes multiple sensor inputs to detect anomalies and classify them into specific fault categories for targeted maintenance actions.

Comprehensive Predictive Maintenance Solution

Our platform integrates these three specialized models to provide a holistic predictive maintenance solution. Each model was carefully selected and trained based on extensive research into the most effective approaches for detecting specific failure modes in industrial equipment.

From data collection methodologies to model training and evaluation, every aspect of our system is designed for accuracy and reliability. We provide detailed information about our data collection processes, model selection rationale, and comprehensive evaluation metrics to ensure transparency and build trust in our predictions.
